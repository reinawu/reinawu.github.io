Is it possible to pull 1.4 billion Chinese people into a WeChat group technically?



"Recently, there is a very hot question on Zhihu:" Is it possible to pull 1.4 billion Chinese people into a WeChat group technically? "

In fact, according to data from the National Bureau of Statistics, as of the end of 2017, the total population of mainland China was 1,390.8 million (including 31 provinces, autonomous regions, municipalities, and Chinese military servicemen, excluding Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan, and overseas Chinese Number), already more than 1.3 billion.

At present, the maximum number of WeChat group members is 500, pulling nearly 1.4 billion Chinese into a WeChat group. Is the reality considered from a technical perspective? How many servers are needed? And in a group of 1.4 billion people, how to grab red envelopes? Will the phone explode?

This question blew up Tencent's official answer: Let me talk about the conclusion first: maybe it can be achieved, but you will see nothing.

According to the public data of the "WeChat Data Report" in 2017 [Reference 1]: In September 2017, WeChat logged an average of 902 million people a day and sent messages 38 billion times a day.
